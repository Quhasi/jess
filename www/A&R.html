Advices and Recommendations

Nutrition Advices and Recommendations

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Avoid foods high in sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats.
  • Take a prenatal vitamin with folic acid to support fetal development.
  • Include iron-rich foods like lean meats, spinach, and fortified cereals.

Exercise Recommendations

  • Engage in moderate-intensity exercise like brisk walking or swimming for 30 mins a day.
  • Avoid high-impact exercises that pose a risk to your joints.
  • Consider prenatal yoga or Pilates for flexibility and relaxation.
  • If you experience pain or bleeding, stop and consult your doctor immediately.

Health Tips

  • Attend regular prenatal check-ups to monitor your baby's development.
  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep per night to help your body grow.
  • Maintain good posture to avoid back pain and discomfort.
  • Manage stress through deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.

Safety Precautions

  • Limit caffeine: Consume less than 200mg per day.
  • Avoid harmful substances: Don't smoke or drink alcohol.
  • Avoid lifting heavy objects that can strain your back.
  • Wear comfortable shoes that provide good support and stability.
